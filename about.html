Few About Our Hotel


Offering a terrace and sea views, Two Mermaids is located in Ksamil, just a short walk from the nearest beach. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant.
Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and features a balcony.

A flat-screen TV is provided in each room, while a private bathroom fitted with a shower.
